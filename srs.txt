[ AXIS ]




Description of the concept of their Mission; 
My mission statement is to improve the quality of education in high school by changing the learning experience through the application of different learning delivery & experience and a change in student-teacher dynamics to foster a better environment and overall experience for the students.

Problem statement: 
 Students are experiencing a lack of engagement and information retention from how the education system is structured combined with ineffective student-teacher dynamics  this in turn affects them after graduation in finding employment and pursuing further education

Software development model 

The Agile development model was selected for AXIS because the system is highly interactive and user focused , which means that it requires continuous improvement based on user feedback being the student and teachers features such as gamified learning, feedback integration and career pathways that  allow students to visualize their future and benefit from the incremental development and regular review that agile incorporates , with each iteration focusing on planning core features refining design and analyzing outcomes before the next cycle


The hypothesis of their solution
I anticipate that there will be an increase in engagement  and participation once the project has gained traction in a period of 5 years  and is already in use and due to the use of gamified lessons and continuous feedback loops students are expected to show improvement in the motivation department and a clear understanding of how their academics translate to real world jobs

1. 	Introduction
1.1 	Purpose
This Software Requirements Specification (SRS) outlines the project for a gamified Learning Management System designed to improve engagement among high school students in Africa. The primary goal is to ensure they are gaining skills that will translate fluently to the real world all while enjoying the study process
By Using Available Technology, this System strives to:
Improve the learning process in high school students
Increase the level of engagement at which students operate
Change the student teacher dynamic
1.2 	Document Conventions
The SRS maintains a simple and consistent formatting style throughout the document. It uses standard font sizes , black text, and Arial font for all text without employing specific typographical conventions . In terms of priorities, there are no distinct priorities to individual requirements. Instead , The document follows a straightforward and uniform approach to present the software requirements

1.4 	Product Scope
Axis is a gamified Learning Management System designed to improve student engagement in high schools changing the traditional student-Teacher dynamics into an interactive , feedback driven learning experience.  The platform provides continuous challenges, badges, and progress tracking to motivate consistent effort and active participation. Axis also provides a space to develop real world skills by providing a career mapping feature that helps students understand how acquired skills align with potential career pathways.
2. 	Overall Description
2.1 	Product Perspective

The software project, “Axis” , is a passion project that aims to increase engagement in high schools , change the teacher-student relationship and equip students with real world skills . The project is a standalone and is not intended to replace any already existing system, it is simply a new way to learn.

The diagram shows the core components of axis which will have three modules with the main one being gamified lms where there will be achievements in form of badges second module being feedback&engagement where the teachers will review and give feed back to the students and the third  will focus on showing career paths to the students
2.2 	Product Functions
User Management : Users can register and log into the system and there will be different types of users such as student teacher and admin
Gamified Learning Management : Teachers will be able to create courses and learning activities, track student progress , experience points and achievements as well as award badges
Feedback and Engagement : Allows both Students and Teachers to give feedback to their peers on lessons  and past assignments while crating a space where continuous feedback can be given while being constructive feedback
Career Linked Learning Pathways: Shows students what each skill acquired translates to in the real world and in the job market and shows the progression of said student in a given career pathway of their choice
Progress Tracking : This allows for easy academic performance and engagement data to be collected in order to improve the project in the areas that have the least engagement while enabling teachers to monitor the students progress and in turn the students can see how they are progressing
Data management : Stores and manages user, learning,feedback and progress datat while Ensuring data consistency, availability and reliability across the system

2.3 	User Classes and Characteristics
Axis is a software product designed to be used by multiple users with different uses for it , each having different characteristics and roles  in the running of Axis , the use classes are separated by how they interact with the project.
Students :
Characteristics:  high school student that uses axis daily to  weekly for studying 
Roles : used as a metric of success based on how many students are using the platform and how high engagement is
Teachers : 
Characteristics: Users uses Axis to make learning content , track student progress and provide feedback
Roles : To create and manage courses as well as providing feedback and guidance for the students
Admins :
Characteristics : User  has full system access to the system and works to uphold system integrity
Roles : Maintains the platform, manages user accounts and system maintenance
Academy Supervisors:
Characteristics : User is a part of the school administration and uses axis to a gain insights for academic performance
Roles : To use Axis and see the impact on the student body and in turn give a review on the system
